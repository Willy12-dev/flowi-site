# The Flowi Family — Character Bible
## The face of Flowi Group across all platforms

---

## THE CONCEPT

The Flowi Family is a Kenyan family whose daily life, drama, and hustle IS the Flowi Group brand. Each family member represents a different product. The content is entertainment first, business second — people follow for the family, stay for the products.

Target audience: Americans (primary), African diaspora, global English-speaking audience.
Platforms: TikTok, IG Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts, X (text only)
Format: 30-60 second episodes, recurring characters, storylines that build over time

---

## THE CHARACTERS

### MAMA FLOWI — "The Builder"
**Real name:** Grace
**Age:** Late 30s
**Represents:** Flowi Agency (useflowi.app)
**Look:** Beautiful Kenyan woman. Natural hair or styled. Smart-casual — blazer over a nice top. Always looks put-together. Warm but firm.
**Hedra prompt:** "Professional Kenyan woman, late 30s, warm smile, natural hair, wearing a navy blazer over white top, clean background, confident posture, studio lighting"

**Personality:**
- The glue of the family. Runs everything.
- Always building websites, chatbots, making AI videos
- Constantly on her laptop or phone
- Proud of her work. Shows it off.
- Gets frustrated with the kids but loves them
- Voice of reason when Dad goes too far
- Catchphrase: "If you have time to complain, you have time to build."

**Content style:**
- Shows off client websites she just built
- Demos AI chatbots she created
- Reacts to bad websites ("Let me fix this...")
- Gives business advice to the kids
- Lectures the daughter about focus

---

### BABA FLOWI — "The OG"
**Real name:** James
**Age:** Early 40s
**Represents:** Flowi Group (the umbrella, the authority)
**Look:** Strong Kenyan man. Clean-shaven or short beard. Polo shirt or button-down. Sometimes in a traditional Kenyan shirt. Dad energy.
**Hedra prompt:** "Kenyan man, early 40s, strong build, short beard, wearing a dark polo shirt, serious but warm expression, clean background, studio lighting"

**Personality:**
- Traditional Kenyan father. Strict but wise.
- Doesn't fully understand the tech but respects the hustle
- Always comparing everything to "back in Kenya"
- Suspicious of the son's crypto ("That is not real money")
- Worried about the daughter ("Who is this boy texting you?")
- Proud of Mama's agency work — he's the one who pushed her to start
- Drops wisdom bombs randomly
- Catchphrase: "In Kenya, we didn't need AI. We had common sense."

**Content style:**
- Reacts to what the kids are doing
- Gives unsolicited life advice
- Argues with the son about crypto vs real business
- Interrogates the daughter's phone
- Supports Mama's business — shares her wins
- Represents Flowi Group values: discipline, hard work, family

### JAYDEN — "The Trader"
**Real name:** Jayden (Jay)
**Age:** Early 20s
**Represents:** Flowi Capital (flowicapital.com) + TrendyAirdrops (trendyairdrops.com)
**Look:** Young Kenyan-American guy. Fresh cut, streetwear — hoodie, chain, AirPods always in. Looks like he's always checking his phone.
**Hedra prompt:** "Young Kenyan-American man, early 20s, fresh haircut, wearing a black hoodie with gold chain, AirPods in one ear, confident smirk, clean background, studio lighting"

**Personality:**
- Always on his phone checking charts
- Talks about crypto, airdrops, trading 24/7
- Thinks he's smarter than everyone
- Actually makes money sometimes — which shocks Dad
- Gets humbled regularly by the market
- Tries to get Dad to invest ("Just $500, Dad, trust me")
- Competitive with his sister
- Catchphrase: "Dad, this isn't gambling. It's INVESTING."

**Content style:**
- Shows trading wins (and losses for comedy)
- Explains crypto/airdrops in simple terms
- Argues with Dad about modern finance
- Tries to convince Mom to accept crypto payments on her sites
- Promotes Flowi Capital and TrendyAirdrops naturally through his lifestyle

**Age:** Late teens / early 20s
**Represents:** Woyuduin (woyuduin.com)
**Look:** Pretty Kenyan-American girl. Stylish — trendy outfits, nails done, always on her phone texting. The kind of girl who takes 50 selfies.

## FAMILY DYNAMICS

**Mom + Dad:** Classic African couple. Mom is the modern one (tech, AI, business). Dad is traditional (values, discipline, hard work). They balance each other. They argue but love each other.

**Mom + Son:** She respects his hustle but wishes he'd focus on "real" business. Tries to get him to help with the agency.

**Mom + Daughter:** Frustrated but loving. "I built an entire app because of you." Wants her to focus.

**Dad + Son:** Classic clash. Dad thinks crypto is a scam. Son thinks Dad is old-school. But Dad secretly checks the charts when nobody's looking.

**Dad + Daughter:** Overprotective African father. "Who is this boy?" Checks her phone. Doesn't approve of any boyfriend. Comedy gold.

**Son + Daughter:** Sibling rivalry. He thinks she's wasting her life. She thinks he's a nerd. They roast each other constantly. But they have each other's back.

## RECURRING THEMES

1. **Family dinner conversations** — everyone at the table, each promoting their thing naturally
2. **Dad discovers something** — finds son's trading account, daughter's texts, Mom's AI video of herself
3. **Mom shows off work** — "Look what I built today" → family reacts
4. **Son tries to recruit family** — "Just buy this one coin" → Dad's reaction
5. **Daughter gets caught** — phone drama, boy drama, Mom/Dad react
6. **Family vs. the world** — all working together on something, showing Flowi Group unity
7. **Culture clash** — Kenyan values vs American lifestyle, always funny
8. **Success moments** — someone in the family wins, everyone celebrates (but also roasts)

---

## CROSSOVER CONTENT

The magic is when products cross over naturally:
- Mom builds a website for a client → Son says "accept crypto payments" → Dad says "accept real money" → comedy + promotes both Flowi Agency and Flowi Capital
- Daughter is distracted → Mom installs Woyuduin on her phone → promotes the app through story
- Son loses money trading → Dad says "should've built websites like your mother" → promotes agency
- Family dinner → everyone pitches their product → Dad just wants to eat in peace

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Generate each character on Hedra with consistent photos/prompts
- Use the SAME photo for each character every time (consistency = recognition)
- Scripts should be 30-60 seconds max
- One character per video OR family interaction (max 2-3 characters)
- Post same video across TikTok, IG, FB Reels, YT Shorts
- X gets text-only versions or screenshots from the videos
- Build storylines over weeks — people come back for the next episode
- Mix standalone videos (one character, one topic) with family episodes
