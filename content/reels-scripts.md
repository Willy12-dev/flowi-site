## PRODUCTION TIPS

- **Film in portrait (9:16)** for all platforms
- **First 3 seconds are everything** — use a strong hook or visual
- **Text overlays** on every clip — most people watch muted
- **Screen recordings:** Use your phone recording the laptop, or direct screen capture
- **Face on camera** for at least 2-3 reels per week — builds trust
- **Post time:** 7-9 AM or 7-10 PM EAT (when Kenyans are scrolling)
- **Frequency:** Minimum 1 reel/day, ideally 2-3
- **Cross-post:** Same video on IG, TikTok, Facebook Reels, and YT Shorts
